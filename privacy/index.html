Privacy Policy

What We Collect

When you submit a request through our contact form, we collect:

  • First name and last name
  • Work email address
  • Company name
  • A description of your biggest close bottleneck

How We Collect It

Data is collected through a form on our Submit a Request page. The form is powered by Formspree, a third-party form processing service that receives and stores your submission before forwarding it to us.

How We Use It

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your inquiry and determine whether there is a fit for working together. We do not use your data for marketing, sell it to third parties, or share it with anyone beyond Formspree's processing role.

Third-Party Processors

Formspree acts as our data processor for form submissions. You can review their privacy practices at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

Cookies, Analytics, and Tracking

This website does not use cookies, analytics tools, or any tracking technologies. We do not collect behavioral data, IP-based analytics, or any information beyond what you explicitly submit through the form.

Data Retention

We retain your submission for as long as needed to respond to your inquiry and evaluate a potential engagement. If you'd like your data deleted, contact us and we'll remove it promptly.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or want to request deletion of your data, reach out via our contact form or email us at privacy@tetra.consulting.
